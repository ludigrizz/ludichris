About Me

Hi, I’m Chris. This is my little corner of the internet for thoughts, blog posts, playlists, screenshots, references, and things I want to keep.

I like night drives, neon colours, weird internet nostalgia, soft chaos, music that feels cinematic, and writing things down before they disappear.

This site is part diary, part moodboard, part archive. It is where I put the pieces of myself that don’t fit anywhere else.

You can read my posts, listen to what I’m looping lately, or message me if you want to talk.
